# Finding the Play Button in Xcode

## Location

The Play button (▶️) is at the **top left of the Xcode window**, in the toolbar.

## If You Don't See It

1. **Show the Toolbar**:
   - Go to **View → Show Toolbar** (or press **Cmd + Option + T**)
   - The toolbar should appear at the top

2. **Look for these buttons** (from left to right):
   - **Stop** button (⏹️) - stops running builds
   - **Play** button (▶️) - builds and runs
   - **Scheme selector** - shows your app name (UseYourTools)
   - **Device selector** - shows your device (Morrígan)

## Alternative: Use Keyboard Shortcut

Instead of clicking the Play button, you can press:
- **Cmd + R** - Build and Run
- **Cmd + B** - Build Only

## Alternative: Menu Bar

1. Click **Product** in the menu bar (top of screen)
2. Click **Run** (or press **Cmd + R**)
